The extensions mhchem and copy-tex are also included (along the necessary auto-render). The mhchem extension allows chemistry notation, like \(\ce{H2O}\) or: $$ \ce{x Na(NH4)HPO4 ->[\Delta] (NaPO3)_x + x NH3 ^ + x H2O} $$ While copy-tex allows users to copy the rendered KaTeX and paste it in its native format. It’s worth noting, though, that copy-tex has some known issues. About how to load the extensions, here’s a quick example: --- title : "More KaTeX" katex : true katexExtensions : [ mhchem, copy-tex ] --- ...
